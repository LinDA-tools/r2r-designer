    The App

    The ‘r2rDesignerApp’ holds the module and it’s configuration for the r2r-designer.

    'use strict'
  • ¶

    These are the dependencies of the app:

    app = angular.module 'r2rDesignerApp', [
  'ngCookies',
  'ngResource',
  'ngSanitize',
  'ngRoute',
  'ui.bootstrap',
  'duScroll',
  'underscore',
  'angularFileUpload'
]
  • ¶

    The routes of the app are configured to listen to /csv (default) and /rdb. You can name distinct controllers to handle the routes, but we will use the same (largely empty) MainCtrl.

    app.config ($routeProvider) ->
  $routeProvider.when '/csv',
    templateUrl: 'partials/main.html'
    controller: 'CsvCtrl'
  .when '/rdb',
    templateUrl: 'partials/main.html'
    controller: 'RdbCtrl'
  .otherwise
    redirectTo: '/csv'
